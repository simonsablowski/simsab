Enabling extraordinary team performance

My principles

I enable teams to radically increase their performance and help people to become deeply satisfied with their work by strengthening collaboration and communication and fostering continuous learning and improvement.

Self-organising teams who work for a compelling purpose are most likely to generate innovation and products must be developed with an approach designed around frequent feedback to truly please the customers.

Highly motivated people who take pride in their work create inspiring products when they are trusted with their capability to find solutions to real problems, and when they regularly reflect on how to become more effective.

2009 - 2011
Co-founder and CTO at Motivado

2.5 years

Motivado is the first platform for interactive video coaching and assists people in finding solutions for personal issues and reaching goals. Motivado closes the gap between cost-intensive personal coaching and the general guidebook market with an approach of mass customisation for coaching content.

I co-founded Motivado and was part of a cross-functional Scrum team of diversely skilled people ranging from software developers to psychologists. In a highly agile fashion, we shipped new product increments iteratively and incrementally sprint after sprint, continuously optimised our processes and paired in order to find appropriate solutions for problems and improving our products.

Motivado's software components include a Magento-based coaching store, a JavaScript-based application for configuring coachings, a PHP-based database importer for coaching content, a JSON API for delivering coaching content as well as a Flex-based frontend for displaying the video-based coaching content. Our innovative technologies have been awarded by Germany's Federal Ministry for Economy and Technology.
